Local excision can be a definitive treatment for T1 SM1 N0 rectal adenocarcinomas, but post-excision histology may reveal adverse features or more advanced tumour stage, requiring adjuvant treatment.
For patients who are unfit or unwilling to undergo invasive surgery, Contact X-ray Brachytherapy (CXB) boost and external beam (chemo)radiotherapy [EB(C)RT] can be used as an alternative option to reduce recurrence risk.
