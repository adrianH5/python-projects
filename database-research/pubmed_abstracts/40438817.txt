Acute lymphocytic leukemia (ALL) is a malignancy of infiltration of B or T precursor lymphoid cells, i.e., lymphoblasts, in bone marrow and other lymphoid organs.
Detection of various cytogenetic abnormalities in ALL patients is important for the purpose of prognosis and targeted treatment.
The aim of this study is to identify the type and frequency of various cytogenetic abnormalities in adolescent and young adult precursor B-cell ALL (B-ALL) patients in Pakistan and compare them with patients of a similar age group in other European and Asian countries.
