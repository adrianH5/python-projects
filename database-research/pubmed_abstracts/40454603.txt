The increasing resistance of cancer drugs, especially in the treatment of breast cancer, underlines the urgent need for new and effective cancer drugs.
Chemotherapeutic agents based on metal complexes are recognized as highly effective treatment options.
The aim of this study is to synthesize a non-platinum chemotherapeutic agent with high efficacy and to investigate its therapeutic effect in mice with tumors.
