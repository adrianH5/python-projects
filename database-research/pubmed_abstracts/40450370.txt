Thyroid cancer, characterized by significant genetic and epigenetic alterations, remains a critical focus of molecular oncology.
This study investigates eight key genes (BRAF, EIF1 AX, FOXE1, KRAS, PDGFRA, PIK3 CA, PTEN, and TERT) that are deregulated in Hashimoto's Thyroiditis and their roles in thyroid cancer.
