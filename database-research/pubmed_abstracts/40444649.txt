Obesity is a growing global health concern and poses significant challenges in rectal cancer surgery.
Excess visceral fat can obscure surgical landmarks, complicate dissection, and increase the risk of intraoperative adverse events (iAEs).
Despite these recognized difficulties, there is limited objective data quantifying the impact of obesity on intraoperative complications.
