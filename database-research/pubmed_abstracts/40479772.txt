Postoperative neck hemorrhage (PNH) and subsequent hematoma formation are well-documented complications of head and neck surgery that can result in severe airway narrowing.
A rapid response from co-medical staff to surgeons is critical in life-threatening situations.
For this purpose, we created an online training video to help nurses easily grasp key points from the consensus statement on PNH management issued by the Japanese Center for Medical Safety and Assistance.
