Meningiomas are among the most common types of intracranial neoplasm.
Sphenoorbita meningioma usually affect anatomical and functional around the orbits.
This study aimed to analyse the epidemiological distribution and clinical data of patients with sphenoorbital meningioma who underwent tumor resection microsurgery with a focus on surgical outcomes.
Patients and Methodo: This is a retrospective descriptive study conducted in Dr. Cipto Mangunkusumo Hospital as a national referral hospital in Indonesia between 2018 and 2023.
Epidemiological data and clinical status were collected through medical record.
