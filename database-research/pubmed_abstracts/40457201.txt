Early-Onset Gastrointestinal Cancers concerns tumors in people under 50.
Accumulating evidence suggests a significant increase in the burden of colorectal cancer in young adults.
We investigated the global burden and spatiotemporal trends of the 6 major gastrointestinal (GI) cancers, including colon and rectum cancer (CRC), esophageal cancer (EC), gallbladder and biliary tract cancer (GBTC), liver cancer (LC), pancreatic cancer (PC), and stomach cancer (SC).
