Endometrial hyperplasia (EH) is a gynecological disease characterized by pathological overgrowth of the endometrium as a result of the unhindered action of estrogens.
The relevance of studying this disease is based on the morbidity rate of this disease in women in the Republic of Kazakhstan, which, in the absence of timely diagnosis, treatment and dynamic monitoring, may lead to the rapid progression to endometrial cancer (EC).
