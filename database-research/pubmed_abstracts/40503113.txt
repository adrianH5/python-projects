Hypopharyngeal perforation in a case of carcinoma hypopharynx is very rare presentation and can present with deep neck space collection and surgical emphysema.
No such case of spontaneous hypopharyngeal perforation in carcinoma hypopharynx has been reported till date to the best of our knowledge.
In this case series, we report 3 such cases of Carcinoma hypopharynx with spontaneous hypopharyngeal perforation which was managed surgically.
