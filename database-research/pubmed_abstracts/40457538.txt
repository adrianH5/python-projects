Many Chinese patent drug and Chinese herbal formulas containing Paris polyphylla Smith var.
chinensis are frequently utilized as complementary treatments in cancer, aiming to enhance efficacy and mitigate adverse reactions.
Recent studies highlight the crucial function of herbal polysaccharides in anti-tumor and immunomodulation activity.
Yet, the specific impact of polysaccharides from Paris polyphylla (PPP) on tumor and immunity remains unclear.
