Between 2020 and 2022, Pakistan's population reached 235.7 million, with an average of 120.6 million men and 115.1 million women each year.
Balochistan is in the southwest of Pakistan and is the fourth most populous region in the country, with an average annual population estimated at 13.9 m. There were 6.8 m (48.7%) children, 1.4 m (10.3%) adolescents, and 5.7 m (40.9%) adults over 2020-2022.
The male population (7.4 m (52.6%)) was higher than the female population (6.6 m (47.4%)).
