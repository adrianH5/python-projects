Patient-reported outcome measures (PROMs) are valuable tools for evaluating outcomes of healthcare interventions and have been increasingly used in health systems around the world.
While PROMs adoption has grown globally, variations exist in their use across the health system.
This stakeholder engagement and consultation activity aims to understand factors impacting PROMs adoption, implementation, and utilization, and identify strategies for enhancing utilization of PROMs data within the healthcare system in Alberta, Canada.
