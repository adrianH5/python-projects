Cancer is emerging as a leading cause of morbidity and mortality in India and other countries worldwide.
Preventable risk factors for cancer, such as the use of tobacco and alcohol, unhealthy diets and physical inactivity, are often initiated and established during adolescence.
Identifying effective strategies for engaging adolescents in cancer-protective behaviours is essential.
The current study investigates the potential of schools as settings and school curricula as tools for raising cancer awareness among teenagers.
