Glutaredoxin 2 (Grx2) is a member of the glutaredoxin family of proteins, which maintain cellular thiol homeostasis.
Grxs is reported to be involved in tumor progression in various cancers.
However, the expression and role of Grx2 in colon cancer remain unexplored.
This study aimed to clarify the relationship between Grx2 expression and tumor progression in colon cancer.
