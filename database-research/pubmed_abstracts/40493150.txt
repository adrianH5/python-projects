Endoscopic incision (EI) combined with triamcinolone (TAA) injection has emerged as an effective method for treating benign esophageal stricture (BES).
However, a single EI and TAA injection seems to be insufficient for long-term relief of BES in some patients, especially those with recurrent BES.
Here, we aimed to investigate the long-term efficacy and safety of repeated EIs and TAA injections in patients with recurrent BES.
