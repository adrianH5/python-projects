Without histological analysis, the diagnosis and grading of ocular surface squamous neoplasia (OSSN) are challenging in clinical differentiation between malignant and premalignant lesions.
This study examined EZH2 expression in corneal and conjunctival epithelial benign hyperplasia, intraepithelial neoplasia (CIN) and squamous cell carcinoma (SCC), and evaluated its relationship with clinicopathological features for diagnostic value.
