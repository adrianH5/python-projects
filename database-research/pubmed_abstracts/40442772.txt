TFE3-rearranged perivascular epithelioid cell tumor (PEComa)-like neoplasm is a recently recognized mesenchymal tumor with melanocytic immunophenotype and TFE3 gene rearrangement, but often lacking smooth muscle differentiation.
Herein, we present a case of TFE3-rearranged PEComa-like neoplasm of the kidney to expand its clinicopathological characteristics and biological behavior.
