BKidney cancer accounts for 5% of all malignant tumors in men.
Worldwide, 90% of kidney malignancies are renal cell carcinomas, while only less than 10% are transitional cell carcinomas.
To our knowledge, only one small study conducted in Lebanon on 124 cases found that more than 20% of the renal cancers were of urothelial pathology, larger epidemiological studies are warranted.
