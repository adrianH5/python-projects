This study evaluated the relationship between 18F-fluorodeoxyglucose PET/computed tomography (18F-FDG PET/CT) radiomic features and clinical parameters, including tumor localization, histopathological subtype, lymph node metastasis, mortality, and treatment response, in esophageal cancer (EC) patients undergoing chemoradiotherapy and the predictive performance of various machine learning (ML) models.
