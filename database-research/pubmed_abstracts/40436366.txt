The overweight/obesity in dogs is defined as a condition of abnormal fat accumulation or, beyond that, a multifactorial condition involving excessive fat accumulation along with different factors (insufficient physical activity, genetics (breed), metabolism, the microbiome, etc.).
Preclinical obesity conditions increases the risk of progressing to clinical obesity and developing obesity-associated diseases such as type 2 diabetes, cardiovascular disease, certain cancers, and mental disorders.
