The coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) pandemic caused by the severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2) virus is having a global impact with latest WHO data indicating 21,294,845 cases and 761,779 deaths (WHO COVID-19 Situation Report 209).
The novel coronavirus SARS-CoV-2, the infective agent causing COVID-19, is having a global impact both in terms of human disease as well as socially and economically.
