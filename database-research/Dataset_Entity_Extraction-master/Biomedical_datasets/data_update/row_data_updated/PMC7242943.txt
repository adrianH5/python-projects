Severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2) or COVID-2019 is a new global health challenge which causes severe respiratory complications.
This retrospective study is the first report of epidemiological trends of COVID-19 in Faisalabad, Pakistan.
RNA was extracted using QIAsymphony DSP Virus/Pathogen Midi Kit and real-time PCR was performed to quantify COVID-19.
Our finding showed that overall prevalence of COVID-19 in Faisalabad on April 4th was 17.18% (22 of 128).
Patients belonging to age group 37–47 years were found to be most (45.5%) infected with COVID-19.
